Biography

Michelle Obama, born La Vaughn Robinson, was born on January 17, 1964. Coming from a modest family, her father was a town hall employee and her mother was a housewife, she grew up in the poorest neighborhood of Chicago, South Side . Michelle Robinson returned to Princeton in 1981, before joining the prestigious Harvard Law School and choosing to specialize in marketing and intellectual property. Michelle Robinson graduated in 1988. At the end of her studies, she returned to Chicago and worked for the law firm Sidley Austin, where she met Barack Obamathat it is responsible for training and supervising. Their idyll begins at the cinema in front of "Do the right thing" by Spike Lee. They married in October 1992 and had two daughters: Malia Ann in 1998 and Natasha in 2001.
